An opportunity for UCLA students to showcase their knowledge of engineering, UCLA and some random fun facts by participating in an online jeopardy game over zoom. The purpose of this event is to help engineering students connect with each other and to provide a sense of community in this difficult time, as well as to exhibit some fun facts about UCLA and engineering in general. There will be prizes for first, second and third place!
Format/logistics: Participants will meet over zoom and use an online buzzer to answer Jeopardy questions. The final jeopardy answer and amount wagered will be collected through a google form.
Prizes: Amazon E-gift cards. $100, $75 and $50 respectively for first, second and third place.

RSVP: https://tinyurl.com/eweekjeopardy
